# Recipes

Assorted notes on food prep.

protein ice cream
- 150mL almond milk
- 60g protein
- 300g fruit (any of strawberry, blueberry, cherry, pineapple)
    - not a fan of kiwi, pineapple, orange is hard


regular ice cream
- earl grey
	- 240mL milk (full fat), 240mL almond milk
	- steep earl grey (2 tsp?)
	- 80g agave (consider subbing for equivalent in cane sugar)
	- 1 tsp vanilla paste
	- 1/2 tsp guar gum
- sesame
	- 240mL milk (full fat), 240mL almond milk
	- 30-60g black sesame (toasted on medium heat)
	- 40g agave (see above, consider subbing)
	- 1 tsp vanilla paste
	- 1/2 tsp guar gum
- earl grey sesame (1:1 ratio of the above)
